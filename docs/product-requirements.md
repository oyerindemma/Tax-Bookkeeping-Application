# Product Requirements

## Core Functional Scope
1. Onboarding and Identity
- Phone and email registration
- Lightweight KYC: name, business type, location
- User classification: individual, SME, informal business
- Guided onboarding with examples

2. Income Recording
- Manual entry (daily sales, lump sums)
- Income source categorisation
- Offline entry with automatic synchronisation
- Optional receipt/photo capture
- Daily, weekly, and monthly summaries

3. Expense Tracking
- One-tap expense logging
- Configurable categories (rent, fuel, stock, utilities, salaries)
- Receipt/photo attachment
- Recurring expense automation

4. Automated Bookkeeping
- Real-time profit and loss
- Cashflow summaries
- Visual dashboards (charts and trends)
- Plain-language explanations of financial status

5. Tax Engine
- Auto-detection of applicable tax types by user profile and location
- Tax estimates based on recorded activity
- Period-based summaries (monthly and annual)
- Scenario simulations (what-if analysis)
- Clear explanations of obligations

6. Compliance and Reporting
- Auto-generated tax reports
- Downloadable statements and ledgers
- Audit-ready summaries and history
- Export to PDF and Excel

7. Reminders and Alerts
- Deadline notifications
- Payment reminders
- Missing data prompts
- Compliance readiness score

8. Education Module
- Bite-sized tax education
- Rights and responsibilities
- Penalty avoidance guidance
- Trust-building content

9. Payments (Future-Ready)
- Bank transfer initiation and reconciliation
- Mobile money support
- Government payment gateway integration where available
- Payment tracking and confirmation

10. Admin and Back Office
- User management console
- Tax rule update engine
- Analytics and usage insights
- Fraud and risk flags
- Secure cloud storage and retention policy

## MVP Definition
The MVP will include:
- Onboarding, income, expenses, and core bookkeeping
- Basic tax estimation and reporting
- Reminders and compliance tracking
- Offline-first sync with conflict resolution
- A minimal education module

## Out of Scope for MVP
- Advanced AI categorisation
- Government API filing integrations
- Cross-border tax support
- Enterprise multi-subsidiary workflows
