# Research Notes and Sources

This document summarises high-level findings that inform product scope, compliance, and security expectations.

## Nigeria Tax Administration (High Level)
- Nigeria has federal and state tax authorities. State Internal Revenue Services administer personal income tax and PAYE for their jurisdictions.
- Federal tax administration is handled by the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS).

Sources:
- Federal Capital Territory IRS overview of Personal Income Tax and PAYE
- Lagos State IRS overview of Personal Income Tax and PAYE
- ZIRS overview of federal and state tax administration

## Data Protection
- Nigeria operates a national data protection regime overseen by the Nigeria Data Protection Commission.

Sources:
- NDPC page on the Nigeria Data Protection Act and its mandate
- NITDA NDPR resources (legacy guidance where still referenced)

## Security and Assurance Standards
- ISO/IEC 27001 defines a formal ISMS framework.
- NIST CSF 2.0 provides a risk management framework for cybersecurity.
- OWASP ASVS and MASVS define application security requirements for web and mobile.
- PCI DSS applies if payment card data is handled.
- SOC 2 defines trust services criteria for enterprise assurance.

Sources:
- ISO summary page for ISO/IEC 27001
- NIST CSF 2.0 publication
- OWASP ASVS and MASVS project pages
- PCI Security Standards Council overview of PCI DSS
- AICPA Trust Services Criteria overview

## Accessibility
- WCAG 2.2 AA is the minimum accessibility compliance target for web experiences.

Sources:
- W3C WCAG 2.2 recommendation
