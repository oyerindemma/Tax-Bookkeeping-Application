# Roadmap

This roadmap will be refined after requirements validation and stakeholder alignment. Timelines are indicative.

## Phase 0: Research and Discovery (Weeks 1-4)
- Extract requirements and user journeys from the vision document
- Define MVP scope and success metrics
- Confirm regulatory and compliance baselines
- Produce UX flows and content style guide (UK spelling)

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 5-10)
- Establish architecture and data model
- Build design system and component library
- Set up CI/CD, environments, and observability baseline
- Implement identity, onboarding, and core ledger models

## Phase 2: MVP Build (Weeks 11-20)
- Income and expense capture (offline-first)
- Bookkeeping summaries and dashboards
- Tax estimation engine v1
- Reporting and exports
- Reminder and education modules

## Phase 3: Beta and Pilot (Weeks 21-26)
- Pilot with selected users
- Validate accuracy and usability
- Performance and security hardening
- Iterate based on feedback

## Phase 4: Enterprise Readiness (Weeks 27-36)
- SOC 2 and ISO 27001 readiness activities
- Advanced audit logging and data retention controls
- Scale testing and disaster recovery drills

## Phase 5: Launch and Expansion (Weeks 37+)
- Wider market release
- Integrations with payment and government systems
- AI-assisted categorisation and guidance
