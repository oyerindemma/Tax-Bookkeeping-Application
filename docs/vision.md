# Vision

## Purpose
Build a trusted, enterprise-grade tax bookkeeping platform for Nigeria that makes compliance simple, affordable, and transparent for individuals, SMEs, and informal-sector businesses.

## Product Objectives
- Remove fear and complexity from taxation with clear guidance and automation.
- Provide accurate, audit-ready bookkeeping without requiring accounting expertise.
- Offer dependable offline-first workflows suitable for low bandwidth environments.
- Uphold strong security, privacy, and data ownership expectations.
- Support future integration with government and payment systems.

## Target Users
- Individuals: salary earners, freelancers, gig workers
- SMEs: service and product businesses
- Informal-sector operators: traders, artisans, transport operators
- Micro-enterprises transitioning into formal taxation

## Design Principles
- Mobile-first and resilient in low connectivity scenarios.
- Extremely simple UX with plain language and guided workflows.
- Automation over manual input where possible.
- Localised language and terminology (with UK spelling).
- Trust and transparency as first-order features.

## Definition of Success
- Users can record income and expenses in under 2 minutes per day.
- Tax obligations are explained clearly, with actionable steps.
- Reports are audit-ready with clear provenance.
- The system demonstrates measurable increases in compliance readiness.
